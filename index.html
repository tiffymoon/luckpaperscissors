About me

It started when my dad bought me and my siblings an origami book when we were growing up in the Philippines, and I remember watching him fold a flapping bird and thinking it was some kind of magic.

I've been folding ever since, quietly, constantly, through Singapore, Dublin, and now Berlin. I made crane mobiles at home. I folded tiny cranes out of receipts whenever I was out.

Luck Paper Scissors started as a blog in 2013. It went quiet for a while. It's back now, with a clearer identity: origami wall art and jewellery. I still fold that bird. My daughter thinks it's magic too.
